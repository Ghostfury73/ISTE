Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national, non-profit making society registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. Our aim is to equip engineers of today with the technical and management skills to solve the challenges of tomorrow by learning and being a part of departments such as technical , electrical, management and design.
Experience an exhilarating Hack-a-thon to cultivate technical expertise and team work, guided by technical evangelists from different sectors, to come up with innovative answers to real world problems.
